About

The scope of "Smart Traffic Control and Violation Detection System" is to develop an intelligent system that addresses challenges related to urban traffic congestion, road safety and prioritizes emergency vehicles for quicker response times. The system leverages advanced technologies to analyze real-time traffic conditions, optimize traffic flow, and enhance compliance with traffic regulations. It includes dynamic signal control to reduce delays and improve efficiency at intersections, along with automated violation detection to identify non-compliance such as red-light breaches and helmet violations. The objective is to create a scalable, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly solution that enhances urban mobility, supports law enforcement efforts, and promotes safer road usage. By integrating real-time traffic density monitoring and automated signal adjustments, the project aims to deliver a robust platform for modern traffic management in growing cities.
